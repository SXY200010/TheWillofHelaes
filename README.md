<b><h1>调整内容</h1></b>

<h3>额外修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li>魔战士法术战斗：开启法术战斗时，近距离使用法术不会引发借机攻击。</li>
</ul>

<h3>背景修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>盖布死灵师</b>：盖布死灵师将学识（宗教）加入本职技能列表。其召唤的不死生物和死灵生物伙伴的攻击检定、AC和先攻获得+2加值，并且自身在使用死灵法术造成的伤害上获得+1加值。自身法术穿透+1，死灵法术DC+1</li>
</ul>

<h3>融血师修改</h3>

<p><b>1级</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>聚能灌注：限制每回合1次，5级时DC加值提升至+2，8级时由迅捷动作变为自由动作。</li>
  <li>恶心灌注：限制每回合1次，恶心持续轮数改为1+法师等级的一半，8级时由迅捷动作变为自由动作。</li>
</ul>

<p><b>5级</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>原本5级获得的法师额外专长延后至6级获得，并固定为增强召唤。</li>
  <li>号令灌注：力量和魅力的增强加值改为天赋加值，加值目标从骷髅改为死灵生物。15级时，以此方式召唤的死灵生物以及不死生物伙伴额外获得数值为三倍你法师等级的临时生命值。</li>
</ul>

<p><b>15级</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>新增亵渎灌注：每回合限1次，为自由动作。下一个被灌注的死灵法术施法者等级获得+2加值。</li>
</ul>

<p><b>20级</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>完美灌注：融血灌注不再消耗生命值，聚能灌注和亵渎灌注的加值提升至+4。</li>
</ul>

<h3>死灵学派能力修改</h3>
<p><b>1级</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>驱散死灵：额外对敌对死灵生物造成1D4+1/2法师等级+智力调整值的伤害。</li>
  <li>墓地之触改名为不死之触：以一个标准动作，你可以碰触一个不死生物并替它注入负能量。使其攻击和豁免检定获得+1亵渎加值，并获得每施法者等级1点的临时生命值。攻击和豁免加值每5个法师等级增加1点。这些加值持续法师等级一半的轮数（至少1轮）。你每天可以使用此能力的次数为3+智力调整值。</li>
</ul>

<p><b>8级</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>生命视野改为亡者视野：你发出一道30英尺范围的灵光，自身与灵光范围内的所有不死生物在属性检定、攻击检定、豁免检定和伤害检定上获得+1亵渎加值；范围内的敌对活物在上述检定上受到-1减值。此外，你自身获得盲视。</li>
</ul>

<h3>骸骨密示域修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>新增启示：唤起死亡</b>：以一个标准动作，唤起一个凶猛骷髅战将来服侍你，这个不死生物的生命值等同于你的先知等级。持续时间等于1分钟/魅力调整值。7级时可以额外唤起一个血腥骨兽，15级时可以额外唤起一个吞魂鬼。</li>
  <li><b>新增启示：血流成河</b>：当你以法术或能力造成负能量伤害时，使敌人开始流血，每轮受到1D6点伤害。5级以及之后每5级，该伤害再增加1D6。这种出血伤害可以通过DC15的医疗检定终止。8级时，你的死灵法术能增加远距离施法，如同运用了超魔：法术及远。</li>
  <li><b>最终启示</b>：增加每轮1次，你能以自由动作使用重伤术或者杀生术。</li>
</ul>

<h3>哀歌咏者修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>坟场奥秘</b>：1环额外获得衰弱射线，2环额外获得恐吓术，3环额外获得次等操纵亡者和剧痛射线，4环额外获得操纵亡者，5环额外获得疲乏波，6环额外获得净化亡者，并且获得6环额外可知法术的等级提前至17级。</li>
  <li><b>鬼魅迭歌</b>：图标换为恐惧术的图标。</li>
  <li><b>尸骸之舞</b>：持续时每轮自动施法一次操纵亡者，次数上限为1/2诗人等级。范围内的不死生物在攻击检定和伤害检定上获得+3表现加值。15级时加值增加至+4，并且使范围内不死生物获得意气风发和大步奔行。20级时加值增加至+5，并且使范围内不死生物获得高等英雄气概和加速术。</li>
</ul>

<h3>战斗祭司死亡领域修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>坟场来客</b>：8级起获得负能量亲和，15级时化为不死生物，获得不死生物面板。</li>
  <li><b>死亡之触</b>：附加的负向等级由1个改为1D4个，目标承受负向等级的持续时间改为1小时。</li>
</ul>

<h3>活尸学家修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>简易赋生</b>：每天1次，额外获得一个召唤CR3僵尸战士的类法术能力（图标使用次等操纵亡者），持续每活尸学家等级10分钟。</li>
  <li><b>精通赋生</b>：每天1次，额外获得一个召唤CR7食尸鬼狩猎大师的类法术能力（图标使用唤起死灵），持续每活尸学家等级10分钟。</li>
  <li><b>高等赋生</b>：每天1次，额外获得一个召唤血腥骨兽的类法术能力（图标使用高等唤起死灵），持续每活尸学家等级10分钟。</li>
</ul>

<h3>牧师亡灵子域修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>死亡之吻</b>：若目标为亡灵生物，可使其在攻击检定、技能检定、属性检定以及豁免检定时获得亵渎加值，加值数和持续轮数等于1/2赋予你此领域的职业等级（最小为1）。</li>
  <li><b>8级死亡拥抱</b>：更改为亡灵之拥，使目标转变为不死生物（轮级BUFF，诅咒效果，可被移除诅咒解除，DC固定为17）。</li>
</ul>

<h3>觅血者修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li>血池点额外获得智力调整值的点数</li>
  <li>腐肉形态时，血池：吸血鬼之触附加及远，如同运用了及远超魔；同时增加迅捷动作版本，并且智力获得+2变形加值。</li>
  <li>若种族为吸血裔，且拥有专长能力饮血者，则每次使用饮血者可恢复1点血池点数，每天恢复不超过血池自身上限。</li>
</ul>

<h3>亡灵血承修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li>改名为亡灵血承。</li>
  <li>3级：惊恐术改为衰弱射线。</li>
  <li>15级：虚体形态增加被动，获得不死生物面板。</li>
  <li>20级：增加对偷袭免疫，并且永久获得不死生物面板。</li>
</ul>

<h3>血怒者亡者血承修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li>4级幽冥之击增加对活物额外造成1D6污邪伤害。</li>
  <li>7级额外法术改为虚假生命，10级额外法术改为吸血鬼之触，13级额外法术改为高等虚假生命。</li>
  <li>血承额外专长删除钢铁意志、健壮、威逼；增加猛力攻击、武器专攻、武器专精（将血怒者等级视为战士等级）、重击精通（依然需要满足专长等级条件）。</li>
  <li>20级亦生亦死增加获得亡灵生物面板。</li>
</ul>

<h3>吸血裔修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li>增加吸血鬼共鸣：施放死灵系法术时施法者等级+1/4人物等级。</li>
  <li>专属专长饮血者改为也可以直接从活物身上吸血。</li>
</ul>

<h3>动物伙伴修改</h3>
<ul>
  <li>所有动物伙伴的死蚀6级额外获得生亡交织，9级额外获得免疫能量流失，15级时被动转变为不死生物面板，并且魅力获得+6亵渎加值。</li>
</ul>

<h3>法术修改：死灵学派</h3>
<p><b>1环</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>惊恐术</b>：目标豁免成功则使其战栗1D4轮。</li>
  <li><b>衰弱射线</b>：增加享受强效、极效和加剧超魔。</li>
  <li><b>恶心射线</b>：若目标已经陷入恶心状态并且再次豁免失败，则使目标陷入反胃状态。</li>
</ul>

<p><b>2环</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>恐吓术</b>：目标豁免成功则使其战栗1D4轮。</li>
  <li><b>号令死灵</b>：改为标准动作施法，持续时间改为1分钟/级，去除每回合豁免摆脱法术的效果。</li>
  <li><b>颤骨</b>：增加对不死生物生效，但是对幽灵类虚体生物依然无效。</li>
  <li><b>食尸鬼之触</b>：增加若目标活物在食尸鬼之触持续时间内被杀死，则将其复活为一具食尸鬼为你战斗，持续时间为1轮/级。</li>
  <li><b>骨拳术</b>：若目标为不死生物，则使其天生防御额外获得+2加值，并且获得1D6+每施法者等级1点（最高10点）临时生命值。</li>
  <li><b>致命毒素</b>：改为中距离施法。目标豁免失败时受到1D3体质伤害，并且在持续时间内，目标进行强韧豁免时额外承受-2减值。</li>
  <li><b>疱疹咒</b>：敏捷属性改为强韧豁免失败时承受4+每2级施法者等级1点减值，豁免成功减半。</li>
</ul>

<p><b>3环</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>力竭射线</b>：若目标已经处于疲劳状态，则无需豁免使其陷入力竭。</li>
</ul>

<p><b>4环</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>降咒</b>：施法改为近距离目标型施法。所有诅咒可重复叠加，属性值最低降至1点。增加恐怖诅咒：每回合目标只有50%概率正常行动，否则它将在原地承受恐惧效果；每回合开始时目标可再次进行意志豁免免除此效果。</li>
  <li><b>疫病术</b>：施法改为近距离目标型施法。增加豁免失败目标在之后每轮都需进行一次豁免以抵御疫病恶化，否则每轮都将再承受一次属性伤害。</li>
  <li><b>碎骨</b>：增加对虚体以外的亡灵生物的伤害骰为D8而非D6。</li>
  <li><b>剧毒之触</b>：改为近距离目标型施法，每轮造成体质伤害改为1D4。</li>
  <li><b>吞噬死灵</b>：增加及远吸血鬼之触的特效。</li>
</ul>

<p><b>5环</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>爆骨</b>：若目标在强韧豁免中失败，将受到每施法者等级8点伤害并且陷入力竭状态，30英尺半径范围内的所有生物受到每施法者等级1D8点伤害（反射豁免检定成功可减半）。如果强韧豁免成功，目标受到每施法者等级1D8点伤害，并且陷入疲劳；30英尺半径范围内的所有生物改为受到每施法者等级1D6点伤害（反射豁免成功可减半）。</li>
  <li><b>剧痛射线</b>：去掉无法将能力值降至1以下的限制。</li>
  <li><b>疲乏波</b>：对于已经疲劳的生物，则使其陷入力竭。</li>
</ul>

<p><b>6环</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>汲取生命</b>：伤害值改为每个施法者等级1D8点，并且使目标陷入疲劳状态。</li>
  <li><b>死亡法阵</b>：生命骰要求从1D4改为1D6（上限20D6）。生命骰小于等于9+每施法者等级1点（最高+10）的生物豁免失败将被立即杀死，生命骰大于该数值的生物豁免失败改为受到100点伤害。</li>
  <li><b>净化亡者</b>：改名不死归亡。数据更改同步死亡法阵：生命骰小于等于9+每施法者等级1点（最高+10）的亡灵生物豁免失败将被立即杀死，生命骰大于该数值的亡灵生物豁免失败改为受到100点伤害。</li>
  <li><b>重伤术</b>：上限提高至20级200点（医疗术同步修改）。</li>
</ul>

<b><h1>Changes</h1></b>

<h3>Extra Changes</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Magus Spell Combat</b>: When Spell Combat is active, casting close-range spells no longer provokes attacks of opportunity.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Background Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Gebian Necromancer</b>: Adds Lore (Religion) to the list of class skills. Undead creatures summoned by the Gebian Necromancer and their necromancy animal companions gain a +2 bonus on attack rolls, AC, and initiative checks. The Gebian Necromancer gains a +1 bonus on damage rolls made with necromancy spells, a +1 bonus to necromancy spell DC, and a +1 bonus on Spell Penetration checks.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Cruoromancer Adjustments</h3>

<p><b>Level 1</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Focused Infusion</b>: Limited to once per round. At 5th level, the DC bonus increases to +2. At 8th level, this infusion becomes a free action.</li>
  <li><b>Sickening Infusion</b>: Limited to once per round. The sickened condition lasts for 1 + half your wizard level rounds. At 8th level, this infusion becomes a free action.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Level 5</b></p>
<ul>
  <li>The wizard bonus feat normally gained at 5th level is delayed to 6th level and is fixed as <b>Augment Summoning</b>.</li>
  <li><b>Commanding Infusion</b>: The Strength and Charisma bonuses are changed from enhancement bonuses to inherent bonuses. The affected target tag is changed from Skeleton to Undead. At 15th level, undead summoned in this way and undead companions affected by it gain temporary hit points equal to three times your wizard level.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Level 15</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Profane Infusion</b> is added: Usable once per round as a free action. The next infused necromancy spell gains a +2 bonus to caster level.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Level 20</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Perfect Infusion</b>: Blood infusions no longer cost hit points. In addition, the bonuses from Focused Infusion and Profane Infusion increase to +4.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Necromancy School Ability Adjustments</h3>
<p><b>Level 1</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Turn Undead</b> additionally deals 1d4 + half your wizard level + your Intelligence modifier damage to hostile undead creatures.</li>
  <li><b>Grave Touch</b> is renamed to <b>Undead Touch</b>: As a standard action, you can touch an undead creature and infuse it with negative energy. The target gains a +1 profane bonus on attack rolls and saving throws, plus 1 temporary hit point per caster level. The bonus on attacks and saves increases by +1 for every 5 wizard levels you possess. These bonuses last for half your wizard level rounds (minimum 1 round). You can use this ability a number of times per day equal to 3 + your Intelligence modifier.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>Level 8</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Life Sight</b> is replaced with <b>Dead Sight</b>: You emit a 30-foot-radius aura. You and all undead within the aura gain a +1 profane bonus on ability checks, attack rolls, saving throws, and damage rolls. Hostile living creatures within the aura take a -1 penalty on those same rolls. Additionally, you gain blindsight.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Bones Mystery Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>New Revelation - Invoke Death</b>: As a standard action, you summon a fierce skeletal champion to serve you. This undead creature's hit points are equal to your oracle level. The duration is 1 minute per Charisma modifier. At 7th level, you can additionally summon one bloody bones beast. At 15th level, you can additionally summon one soul-eater.</li>
  <li><b>New Revelation - Blood Bath</b>: Whenever you deal negative energy damage with a spell or ability, enemies begin to bleed, taking 1d6 damage each round. This damage increases by an additional 1d6 at 5th level and every 5 levels thereafter. This bleed damage can be stopped with a DC 15 Heal check. At 8th level, your necromancy spells gain increased range, as if modified by the Distant Spell metamagic feat.</li>
  <li><b>Final Revelation</b>: Once per round, you may use <b>Harm</b> or <b>Slay Living</b> as a free action.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Dirge Singer Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Secrets of the Grave</b>: Adds the following necromancy spells to the Dirge Singer's known spells: 1st level - Ray of Enfeeblement; 2nd level - Scare; 3rd level - Lesser Control Undead and Ray of Agony; 4th level - Control Undead; 5th level - Wave of Fatigue; 6th level - Purge Undead. The additional 6th-level known spell is also moved earlier to 17th level.</li>
  <li><b>Haunting Refrain</b>: Icon changed to the Fear spell icon.</li>
  <li><b>Dance of the Dead</b>: While active, automatically casts Animate Dead once per round, up to a maximum number of times equal to half your bard level. Undead creatures within the area gain a +3 competence bonus on attack and damage rolls. At 15th level, this bonus increases to +4 and affected undead also gain Good Hope and Longstrider. At 20th level, this bonus increases to +5 and affected undead also gain Greater Heroism and Haste.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Warpriest Death Blessing Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Grave Guest</b>: Starting at 8th level, you gain negative energy affinity. At 15th level, you transform into an undead creature and gain the undead creature type.</li>
  <li><b>Death's Touch</b>: The negative level effect is changed from 1 negative level to 1d4 negative levels. The duration of these negative levels is changed to 1 hour.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Reanimator Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Lesser Animate</b>: Once per day, you additionally gain a spell-like ability that summons a CR 3 zombie warrior. The icon uses Lesser Control Undead. The summon lasts for 10 minutes per Reanimator level.</li>
  <li><b>Improved Animate</b>: Once per day, you additionally gain a spell-like ability that summons a CR 7 ghoul hunt master. The icon uses Animate Dead. The summon lasts for 10 minutes per Reanimator level.</li>
  <li><b>Greater Animate</b>: Once per day, you additionally gain a spell-like ability that summons a bloody bones beast. The icon uses Greater Animate Dead. The summon lasts for 10 minutes per Reanimator level.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Cleric Undead Subdomain Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Death's Kiss</b>: If the target is an undead creature, it gains a profane bonus on attack rolls, skill checks, ability checks, and saving throws. The bonus amount and duration in rounds are equal to half the class level that grants you this domain (minimum 1).</li>
  <li><b>Level 8 Death's Embrace</b>: Replaced with <b>Undead Embrace</b>, which transforms the target into an undead creature. This is a round-per-level buff and a curse effect. It can be removed by Remove Curse, with a fixed DC of 17.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Bloodseeker Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Blood pool points additionally increase by an amount equal to your Intelligence modifier.</li>
  <li>While in carrion form, <b>Blood Pool: Vampiric Touch</b> gains increased range as if modified by the Reach Spell metamagic feat. A swift-action version is also added, and Intelligence gains a +2 polymorph bonus.</li>
  <li>If the character is a dhampir and has the Blood Drinker feat ability, each use of Blood Drinker restores 1 blood pool point. The total points restored per day cannot exceed the blood pool's own maximum.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Undead Bloodline Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Renamed to <b>Undead Bloodline</b>.</li>

<h3>Bloodrager Undead Bloodline Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Level 4 Ghost Strike</b>: Now deals an additional 1d6 unholy damage to living creatures.</li>
  <li>Additional spells changed: 7th level to False Life, 10th level to Vampiric Touch, and 13th level to False Life, Greater.</li>
  <li>Bloodline bonus feats: removed Iron Will, Toughness, and Intimidating Prowess; added Power Attack, Weapon Focus, Weapon Specialization (treating bloodrager level as fighter level), and Improved Critical. Normal feat level prerequisites still apply.</li>
  <li><b>Level 20 One Foot in the Grave</b>: Now also grants the undead creature type.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Dhampir Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Added <b>Dhampir Resonance</b>: When casting necromancy spells, your caster level increases by 1/4 of your character level.</li>
  <li>The exclusive feat <b>Blood Drinker</b> can now also drain blood directly from living creatures.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Animal Companion Adjustments</h3>
<ul>
  <li>All Deathtouched animal companions gain Life and Death Interwoven at 6th level, immunity to energy drain at 9th level, and passively transform into the undead creature type at 15th level. At 15th level, they also gain a +6 profane bonus to Charisma.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Spell Adjustments: Necromancy School</h3>
<p><b>1st-Level Spells</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Cause Fear</b>: On a successful saving throw, the target becomes shaken for 1d4 rounds.</li>
  <li><b>Ray of Enfeeblement</b>: Can now benefit from Empower, Maximize, and Intensified metamagic.</li>
  <li><b>Ray of Sickening</b>: If the target is already sickened and fails the saving throw again, it becomes nauseated instead.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>2nd-Level Spells</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Scare</b>: On a successful saving throw, the target becomes shaken for 1d4 rounds.</li>
  <li><b>Command Undead</b>: Changed to a standard-action spell. Duration changed to 1 minute/level, and the effect no longer allows a new save each round to break free.</li>
  <li><b>Boneshaker</b>: Now affects undead creatures, but still has no effect on incorporeal creatures such as ghosts.</li>
  <li><b>Ghoul Touch</b>: If the living target is slain while Ghoul Touch remains in effect, its corpse rises as a ghoul under your control for 1 round per caster level.</li>
  <li><b>Bone Fists</b>: If the target is undead, it gains an additional +2 bonus to natural armor and receives 1d6 temporary hit points + 1 temporary hit point per caster level (maximum +10).</li>
  <li><b>Pernicious Poison</b>: Changed to medium range. If the target fails its saving throw, it takes 1d3 Constitution damage. For the spell's duration, the target also takes an additional -2 penalty on Fortitude saves.</li>
  <li><b>Pox Pustules</b>: Dexterity effect changed to a penalty of 4 + 1 per 2 caster levels on a failed Fortitude save; a successful save halves the penalty.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>3rd-Level Spells</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Ray of Exhaustion</b>: If the target is already fatigued, it becomes exhausted without requiring a saving throw.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>4th-Level Spells</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Bestow Curse</b>: Changed to a close-range targeted spell. All curses can stack when applied repeatedly, and ability scores can be reduced to a minimum of 1. Added Curse of Terror: each round, the target has only a 50% chance to act normally; otherwise, it remains in place under a fear effect. At the start of each round, the target may attempt another Will save to end this effect.</li>
  <li><b>Contagion</b>: Changed to a close-range targeted spell. If the target fails its saving throw, it must attempt a save each subsequent round to resist the disease worsening; on a failure, it takes the corresponding ability damage again each round.</li>
  <li><b>Boneshatter</b>: Against undead creatures that are not incorporeal, the damage die is changed to d8 instead of d6.</li>
  <li><b>Poison</b>: Changed to a close-range targeted spell. Constitution damage each round is changed to 1d4.</li>
  <li><b>Consume Undead</b>: Added the visual effect from ranged Vampiric Touch.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>5th-Level Spells</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Bone Explosion</b>: If the target fails its Fortitude saving throw, it takes 8 damage per caster level and becomes exhausted; all creatures in a 30-foot radius take 1d8 damage per caster level (Reflex save halves). If the Fortitude save succeeds, the target takes 1d8 damage per caster level and becomes fatigued; all creatures in the radius instead take 1d6 damage per caster level (Reflex save halves).</li>
  <li><b>Wracking Ray</b>: Removed the restriction preventing ability scores from being reduced below 1.</li>
  <li><b>Waves of Fatigue</b>: Creatures that are already fatigued become exhausted instead.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>6th-Level Spells</b></p>
<ul>
  <li><b>Siphon Life</b>: Damage is changed to 1d8 per caster level, and the target also becomes fatigued.</li>
  <li><b>Circle of Death</b>: The affected Hit Dice pool is changed from 1d4 per caster level to 1d6 per caster level (maximum 20d6). Creatures with Hit Dice equal to or less than 9 + 1 per caster level (maximum +10) that fail their saving throw are slain outright. Creatures with more Hit Dice that fail their saving throw take 100 damage instead.</li>
  <li><b>Purge Undead</b>: Renamed to <b>Undeath to Death</b>. Its data is changed to match Circle of Death: undead creatures with Hit Dice equal to or less than 9 + 1 per caster level (maximum +10) that fail their saving throw are slain outright. Undead creatures with more Hit Dice that fail their saving throw take 100 damage instead.</li>
  <li><b>Harm</b>: Maximum damage increased to 200 at caster level 20. Heal is updated accordingly.</li>
</ul>
